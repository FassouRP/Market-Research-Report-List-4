<p><strong>The Instant Coffee Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray-drying</li><li>Freeze-drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Instant coffee is produced using two main methods in the market: spray-drying and freeze-drying. Spray-drying involves spraying a liquid coffee extract into hot air, quickly drying it into a powder form. This process is efficient and cost-effective, making it popular in the mass market. In contrast, freeze-drying involves freezing the coffee extract into ice and then slowly removing the ice through sublimation, resulting in a more flavorful and aromatic coffee. Freeze-dried coffee is considered higher quality but comes with a higher price point, making it popular among premium coffee brands.</p></p>
