<p><p>Swimming pool treadmills are available in two main types - mechanical treadmills and electrodynamic treadmills. Mechanical treadmills use a simple design with a belt that moves as the user walks or runs on it, providing resistance for a cardio workout. On the other hand, electrodynamic treadmills use an electric motor to create resistance in the water, offering a more advanced and customizable workout experience. Both types cater to different preferences and fitness levels.</p></p>
