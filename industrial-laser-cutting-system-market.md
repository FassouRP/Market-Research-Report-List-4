<p><p>The industrial laser cutting system market can be categorized into various types based on the cutting technology used. Laser vaporization cutting involves the use of a high-powered laser beam to vaporize the material, resulting in precise and clean cuts. Laser melting and cutting utilizes a laser beam to heat and melt the material, followed by a high-pressure gas jet to remove the molten material. Laser oxygen cutting involves a chemical reaction between the laser beam and oxygen to cut through the material. Other types of industrial laser cutting systems include laser scribing and ablating.</p></p>
