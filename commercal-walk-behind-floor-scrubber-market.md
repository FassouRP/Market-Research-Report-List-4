<p><p>Commercial walk-behind floor scrubbers can be classified into two main types: manual walk-behind floor scrubbers and automatic walk-behind floor scrubbers. Manual walk-behind floor scrubbers require the operator to physically push the machine while cleaning, making them suitable for smaller areas and allowing for more control over the cleaning process. Automatic walk-behind floor scrubbers, on the other hand, are self-propelled and require less physical effort from the operator, making them ideal for larger spaces and more efficient for continuous cleaning tasks.</p></p>
