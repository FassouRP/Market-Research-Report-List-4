<p><p>Extracorporeal Membrane Oxygenation (ECMO) services are divided into two main types: Veno-Arterial (VA) ECMO services and Veno-Venous (VV) ECMO services. VA ECMO provides both cardiac and respiratory support by pumping blood from a vein to an artery, while VV ECMO primarily focuses on providing respiratory support by circulating blood through an artificial lung. These services are crucial for patients with severe heart or lung failure, providing life-saving treatment outside of the body's natural respiratory and circulatory systems.</p></p>
