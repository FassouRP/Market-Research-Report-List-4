<p><p>Pouch packaging inspection systems refer to the technology used to detect defects in pouch packaging. There are three main types of inspection systems in the market: X-ray, ultrasonic, and visual inspection. X-ray systems are used to detect foreign objects or missing items inside pouches. Ultrasonic systems are employed to check for leaks or seal defects. Visual inspection systems utilize cameras to identify visual defects such as misalignment or printing errors. Each type of system plays a crucial role in ensuring the quality of pouch packaging.</p></p>
