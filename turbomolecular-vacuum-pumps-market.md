<p><p>Turbomolecular vacuum pumps are classified into three main types: magnetically suspended type, oil lubricated type, and others. Magnetically suspended type pumps use magnetic bearings to suspend the rotor, providing high stability and minimal vibration. Oil lubricated type pumps utilize oil for lubrication and sealing purposes. The "others" category may include hybrid pumps or dry pumps. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for different applications based on factors such as speed, contamination sensitivity, and maintenance requirements.</p></p>
