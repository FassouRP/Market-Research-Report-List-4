<p><ul><li>Crawler Machine</li><li>Wheeled Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Small asphalt pavers come in two main types: crawler machines and wheeled machines. Crawler machines are equipped with tracks for improved traction and stability on uneven surfaces, making them ideal for off-road or rough terrain projects. Wheeled machines, on the other hand, are better suited for flat and smooth surfaces due to their maneuverability and ease of transportation. Both types of machines are commonly used in small-scale paving projects such as residential driveways, parking lots, and pathways.</p></p>
