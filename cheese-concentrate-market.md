<p><p>Cheese concentrate market includes two main types: powder and paste. Powder cheese concentrates are dehydrated forms of cheese, typically used in a powdered form for convenience and shelf stability. They are commonly added to snacks, soups, sauces, and seasonings. Paste cheese concentrates, on the other hand, are more creamy and spreadable in texture. They are often used in dips, salad dressings, spreads, and sauces. Both types of cheese concentrates offer a convenient way to add cheesy flavor to a variety of dishes.</p></p>
