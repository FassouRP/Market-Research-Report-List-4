<p><p>Portable metal detectors are commonly categorized into three main types - Pulse Induction, Beat Frequency Oscillations, and Others. Pulse Induction metal detectors are known for their ability to penetrate highly mineralized soil and offer great depth detection, making them suitable for finding deep targets. Beat Frequency Oscillation detectors are more sensitive to smaller metal objects and are popular for their accuracy in discrimination. The category of Others includes various types of metal detectors such as Very Low Frequency detectors, which are user-friendly and commonly used in hobbyist detection.</p></p>
