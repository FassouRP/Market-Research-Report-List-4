<p><p>Holographic plane gratings are used in numerous applications and are available in two main types: transmission gratings and reflective gratings. Transmission gratings allow light to pass through, diffracting it in a specific direction. Reflective gratings redirect light at specific angles by reflecting it off the grating's surface. Both types of gratings have their own unique advantages and are utilized in various industries such as spectrometry, telecommunications, and laser systems for their ability to manipulate light in precise ways.</p></p>
