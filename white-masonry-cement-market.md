<p><p>White masonry cement is commonly categorized into two main types: Type S and Type N. Type S is designed for use in applications requiring high flexural strength, such as structural projects or projects that will be subject to severe weather conditions. Type N is more suitable for general-purpose use, offering good bonding capabilities and workability. Both types of white masonry cement are composed of a blend of white Portland cement, finely ground white limestone, and other additives to improve performance and durability in various construction applications.</p></p>
