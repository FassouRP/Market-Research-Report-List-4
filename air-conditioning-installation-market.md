<p><p>The air conditioning installation market can be divided into two main types: Whole-Home systems and Split Systems. Whole-Home systems involve installing a central unit that cools the entire house through a network of ducts. Split Systems, on the other hand, consist of an outdoor condenser unit and indoor evaporator unit that are connected by refrigerant lines. Split Systems are typically used in homes where ductwork is not feasible or desired, providing targeted cooling for specific areas of the house.</p></p>
