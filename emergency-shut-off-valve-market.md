<p><p>Emergency shut-off valves are crucial for safety in various industries. There are two main types of emergency shut-off valves - motorised valves and solenoid valves. Motorised valves are controlled by an electric motor to quickly shut off flow in case of an emergency, while solenoid valves use a solenoid to control the flow of media. Both types are essential in preventing accidents and protecting equipment from damage in emergency situations. The market for these valves continues to grow as industries prioritize safety measures.</p></p>
