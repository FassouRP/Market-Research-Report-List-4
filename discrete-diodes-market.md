<p><p>Discrete diodes market includes different types such as Schottky diode, Transient Suppression Diode (TVS), Commutation Diode, and others. Schottky diodes are known for their fast switching speeds and low forward voltage drop. TVS diodes are used for suppressing transient voltage spikes. Commutation diodes are used in switching applications. Other types of diodes include Zener diodes, Varactor diodes, and Light Emitting Diodes (LEDs), each serving specific purposes in electronic circuits. The diverse range of diodes caters to various needs in the electronics industry.</p></p>
