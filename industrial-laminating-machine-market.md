<p><p>Industrial laminating machines are categorized into three main types based on the process used for laminating materials. Wet laminating machines involve the use of adhesives to bond layers together, while thermal laminating machines use heat to seal materials together. Dry bond laminating machines utilize pressure-sensitive adhesives for laminating. Each type of machine offers unique advantages and is suited for different applications based on the desired finish and materials being laminated.</p></p>
