<p><p>Solar cell (Photovoltaic) modules are classified into several types based on the materials used in their construction. These types include Mono-Si Modules (monocrystalline silicon), Multi-Si Modules (polycrystalline silicon), CdTe Modules (cadmium telluride), CIGS Modules (copper indium gallium selenide), a-Si Modules (amorphous silicon), and Others. Each type has unique characteristics and efficiencies, with monocrystalline silicon modules being known for their high efficiency and longevity, while thin-film modules like CdTe and CIGS offer flexibility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
