<p><ul><li>Tooth-Supported Overdentures</li><li>Implant-Supported Overdentures</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Overdenture implants come in two main types: tooth-supported overdentures and implant-supported overdentures. Tooth-supported overdentures are attached to remaining natural teeth for support, while implant-supported overdentures are secured onto dental implants that have been surgically placed in the jawbone. Tooth-supported overdentures are a more cost-effective option for patients with some remaining healthy natural teeth, while implant-supported overdentures provide a more stable and long-lasting solution for patients who have lost most or all of their teeth.</p></p>
