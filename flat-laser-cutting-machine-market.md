<p><p>Flat Laser Cutting Machine market can be categorized into two main types: CO2 Flat Laser Cutting Machine and Fiber Flat Laser Cutting Machine. CO2 Flat Laser Cutting Machines utilize carbon dioxide gas to generate the laser beam, making them suitable for cutting non-metal materials like wood, plastic, and acrylic. On the other hand, Fiber Flat Laser Cutting Machines use fiber optic technology to create the laser beam, making them ideal for cutting metal materials such as steel, aluminum, and copper. Both types offer efficient and precise cutting solutions for various industries.</p></p>
