<p><strong>The Handheld Explosives Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trace</li><li>Steam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Handheld explosives detectors are divided into two main types: trace detectors and steam detectors. Trace detectors are used to detect minute traces of explosives particles on surfaces or in the air. They are highly sensitive and can accurately detect a wide range of explosive materials. Steam detectors, on the other hand, use steam to release and detect vapors from explosives, providing a non-contact method of detection. Both types of detectors are essential for protecting against potential threats in various security and military applications.</p></p>
