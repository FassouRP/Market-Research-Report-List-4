<p><p>Portable X-ray equipment for security purposes includes two main market types - CR equipment and DR equipment. </p><p>CR equipment, or computed radiography equipment, uses a cassette-based system to capture x-ray images which are then processed digitally. </p><p>DR equipment, or digital radiography equipment, directly captures and processes x-ray images using a digital detector, eliminating the need for cassettes. </p><p>Both types of equipment offer portable solutions for security checks in various locations, providing quick and efficient scanning of items for potential threats.</p></p>
