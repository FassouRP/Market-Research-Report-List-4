<p><p>UHT cartons are specially designed packaging containers used for Ultra High Temperature (UHT) processing, which sterilizes liquid food products by heating them to very high temperatures for a short period of time. This process ensures the preservation of the food products without the need for refrigeration, allowing for an extended shelf life. UHT cartons are commonly used for packaging dairy products, fruit juices, and other beverages.</p><p>The UHT carton market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing demand for convenient and sustainable packaging solutions.
