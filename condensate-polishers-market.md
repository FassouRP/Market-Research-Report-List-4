There are two main types of Condensate Polishers: Mixed Bed Condensate Polishers and Cation Condensate Polishers. </p><p>Mixed Bed Condensate Polishers use a mixture of cation and anion resin to remove a wide range of impurities, while Cation Condensate Polishers use only cation resin to specifically target positively charged ions. Both types help improve the efficiency and longevity of power plant equipment by maintaining high water quality.</p></p>
