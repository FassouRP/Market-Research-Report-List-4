<p><ul><li>Fixed Size</li><li>Variable Size</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tool holder & collets market has two main types - fixed size and variable size. Fixed size tool holders and collets are designed for specific tool sizes and cannot be adjusted. They offer precision and stability but require changing the holder for different tool sizes. On the other hand, variable size tool holders and collets can accommodate a range of tool sizes, offering flexibility and convenience. However, they may not provide the same level of precision as fixed size holders. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the machining industry.</p></p>
