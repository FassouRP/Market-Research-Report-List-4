<p><p>Smartphone microscopes come in two main market types: Single Tube Microscopes and Multi Tube Microscopes. Single Tube Microscopes are designed to hold and magnify one specimen at a time, providing high resolution images of individual samples. On the other hand, Multi Tube Microscopes are equipped with multiple tubes, allowing for simultaneous observation of multiple specimens or different parts of the same specimen. Both types cater to different user needs and offer varying levels of functionality and versatility in microscopy.</p></p>
