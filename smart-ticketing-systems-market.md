<p><ul><li>Smart Card System</li><li>Open Payment System</li><li>Near-Field Communication System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart ticketing systems consist of three main market types: Smart Card System, Open Payment System, and Near-Field Communication System. The Smart Card System involves pre-loaded cards that users can tap or swipe at entry points. The Open Payment System allows passengers to pay directly using contactless credit or debit cards or mobile payment platforms. The Near-Field Communication System enables users to simply tap their smartphones or wearable devices to access public transportation. These systems aim to provide convenient and efficient ticketing options for users.</p></p>
