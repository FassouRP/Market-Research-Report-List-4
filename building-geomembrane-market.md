<p><ul><li>Single Rough</li><li>Double Rough</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Building Geomembrane market types can be categorized as Single Rough and Double Rough. Single Rough geomembranes have one textured side, providing a rough surface for increased friction and stability. Double Rough geomembranes have two textured sides, offering enhanced grip and protection against slippage. Both types are commonly used in construction projects to prevent seepage and leakage in containment applications such as landfills, ponds, and tunnels. The choice between Single Rough and Double Rough geomembranes depends on the specific requirements of the project.</p></p>
