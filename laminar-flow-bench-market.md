<p><p>Laminar Flow Bench comes in two main market types: Vertical and Horizontal. Vertical laminar flow benches are designed for applications where the user needs a downward stream of clean air for protection from particulates. On the other hand, horizontal laminar flow benches are ideal for applications requiring a horizontal flow of clean air across the work surface. Both types provide a controlled environment for various activities such as laboratory work, pharmaceutical manufacturing, and electronics assembly.</p></p>
