<p><p>Display materials market is dominated by two main technologies - LCD and OLED. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) technology uses liquid crystals and backlighting to produce images, while OLED (Organic Light-Emitting Diode) technology uses organic compounds that emit light when an electric current is passed through them. LCDs are more widespread and cost-effective, while OLEDs offer better picture quality and energy efficiency. Both technologies cater to different market segments with LCDs being popular in TVs and monitors, and OLEDs being preferred in high-end smartphones and TVs.</p></p>
