<p><ul><li>PMOSFET</li><li>NMOSFET</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High speed MOSFET market consists of two main types: PMOSFET and NMOSFET. PMOSFET, or p-channel MOSFET, is a type of MOSFET where the majority charge carriers are holes. It is commonly used in applications where low power consumption is crucial. On the other hand, NMOSFET, or n-channel MOSFET, has majority charge carriers that are electrons and is often used in high-speed switching applications due to its faster switching speeds. Both types play a significant role in the high-speed MOSFET market.</p></p>
