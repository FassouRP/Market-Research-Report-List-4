<p><ul><li>Scalar High-pressure Hydrophone</li><li>Vector High-pressure Hydrophone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High-pressure hydrophones are used to measure sound waves at high pressures underwater. There are two main types in the market - scalar high-pressure hydrophones and vector high-pressure hydrophones. Scalar hydrophones measure pressure variations along a single axis, while vector hydrophones measure pressure variations along multiple axes. Scalar hydrophones are more commonly used for simple applications where only one direction of sound is of interest, whereas vector hydrophones are used for more complex applications where sound waves come from multiple directions.</p></p>
