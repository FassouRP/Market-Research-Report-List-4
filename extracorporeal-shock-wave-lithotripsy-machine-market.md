<p><p>Extracorporeal Shock Wave Lithotripsy (ESWL) machines are used for non-invasive treatment of kidney stones. The three main types of ESWL machines are Electrohydraulic, Piezoelectric, and Electromagnetic. Electrohydraulic machines use an electrical discharge to generate shock waves, Piezoelectric machines use crystals to convert electrical energy into shock waves, and Electromagnetic machines use a coil to create shock waves. Each type of machine has its own advantages and limitations, making them suitable for different clinical settings and patient needs.</p></p>
