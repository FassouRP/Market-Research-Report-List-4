Acrylic sealants offer good adhesion and flexibility, while PVA sealants are water-based and easy to apply. Silicones are resistant to high temperatures and weathering, making them ideal for exterior applications. Polyurethanes are known for their durability and adhesion to a variety of surfaces. Each type of sealant offers unique advantages for automotive applications.</p></p>
