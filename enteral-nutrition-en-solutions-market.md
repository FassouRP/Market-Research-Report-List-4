<p><p>Enteral Nutrition (EN) Solutions are used to provide nutrition directly into the gastrointestinal tract when oral intake is not possible. The market types include Naso Gastric (NG) Feeding, which delivers nutrients into the stomach through a tube inserted through the nose; Naso Duodenal Feeding, which places the tube into the duodenum for more efficient absorption; and Naso Jejunal (NJ) Feeding, where the tube is inserted into the jejunum. Other types of enteral feeding methods may include gastrostomy and jejunostomy tubes.</p></p>
