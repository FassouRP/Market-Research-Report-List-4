<p><p>Semiconductor processing furnaces are used in the production of semiconductor materials. Horizontal furnaces are designed for batch processing and are suitable for high-volume production. They provide uniform heating across a large surface area. Vertical furnaces are ideal for processing small batches or individual substrates and offer precise control over temperature and gas flow. Both types of furnaces play a crucial role in the semiconductor industry by enabling the deposition, diffusion, oxidation, and annealing processes necessary for creating integrated circuits and other electronic devices.</p></p>
