<p><strong>The Automatic Urine Analysis System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Analysis</li><li>Wet Analysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic urine analysis systems are available in two main types: dry analysis and wet analysis. Dry analysis systems use reagent strips to test urine samples for various parameters like pH, glucose, protein, etc. These systems are quick, easy to use, and require minimal human intervention. On the other hand, wet analysis systems involve the use of automated analyzers that perform more sophisticated testing procedures, such as microscopy and chemical analysis. While wet analysis systems offer higher accuracy and a wider range of tests, they are more complex and expensive.</p></p>
