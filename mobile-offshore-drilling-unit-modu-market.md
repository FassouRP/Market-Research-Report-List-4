<p><ul><li>Jackup (Non-Floating)</li><li>Semisubmersible (Floating)</li><li>Drillship (Floating)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mobile Offshore Drilling Units (MODUs) encompass three main types: Jackup (Non-Floating), Semisubmersible (Floating), and Drillship (Floating). Jackup units are fixed on the seabed with extendable legs, suitable for shallow waters. Semisubmersible units float on the water's surface and are stabilized by underwater columns, making them suitable for deeper waters. Drillships are also floating units but have their drilling equipment onboard, allowing for versatile deepwater drilling capabilities. Each type caters to different offshore drilling needs based on water depth and location.</p></p>
