<p><p>Boat exhaust mufflers can be classified into different types based on their design and functionality. Chambered exhaust mufflers use a series of chambers and baffles to reduce noise and turbulence in the exhaust flow. Straight through exhaust mufflers, on the other hand, have a straight tube design that allows for a more efficient flow of exhaust gases. Other types of mufflers in the market may include resonator mufflers, turbo mufflers, or glasspack mufflers, each offering unique features and benefits for boat owners.</p></p>
