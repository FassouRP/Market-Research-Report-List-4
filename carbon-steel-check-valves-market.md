<p><p>Carbon steel check valves are commonly available in two main types in the market: wafer type and lug type. Wafer type check valves are compact and lightweight, designed to fit between flanges, making them easier to install and remove. On the other hand, lug type check valves have threaded inserts on both sides to bolt the valve to the pipeline, providing stability and strength. Both types are commonly used in various industrial applications for controlling the flow of fluids in a single direction.</p></p>
