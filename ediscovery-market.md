<p><ul><li>ECA</li><li>Processing</li><li>Review</li><li>Forensic Data Collection</li><li>Legal Hold Management</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>eDiscovery market types include Early Case Assessment (ECA), Processing, Review, Forensic Data Collection, Legal Hold Management, and Others. ECA helps in analyzing and evaluating case-related data at the early stage of a legal matter. Processing involves the organization and preparation of large volumes of data for review. Review involves the examination and analysis of data for relevance and privilege. Forensic Data Collection involves the gathering of electronic evidence for legal proceedings. Legal Hold Management ensures that relevant data is preserved and not altered. Others refer to additional eDiscovery services and solutions.</p></p>
