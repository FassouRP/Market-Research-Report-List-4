<p><p>Medical dry electrodes are devices used to record bioelectric signals from the human body without the need for conductive gel. <p><p>Medical dry electrodes are available in two main types: disposable and reusable. Disposable electrodes are designed for single-use applications, offering convenience and reducing the risk of cross-contamination. They are commonly used in single-patient settings or for short-term monitoring. Reusable electrodes, on the other hand, are ideal for multiple uses and are more cost-effective in the long run. They are typically made of durable materials that can be easily cleaned and sterilized between uses, making them suitable for long-term monitoring or repeated applications.</p></p>
