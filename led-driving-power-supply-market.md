<p><ul><li>External Power Supply</li><li>Built in Power Supply</li></ul></p>
<p><p>External power supply market refers to LED driving power supplies that are located outside the LED luminaire, providing power through a separate device. Built-in power supply market pertains to LED driving power supplies that are integrated within the LED luminaire itself, eliminating the need for an external power source. Both types of markets cater to different consumer needs and preferences, with external power supplies offering more flexibility in installation and maintenance, while built-in power supplies providing a more streamlined and compact design.</p></p>
