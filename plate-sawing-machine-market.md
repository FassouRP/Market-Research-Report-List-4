<p><p>Plate sawing machines can be divided into two main types: cold sawing machines and hot sawing machines. Cold sawing machines use a toothed blade to cut materials at room temperature, producing clean and burr-free cuts. Hot sawing machines, on the other hand, utilize high temperatures to melt the material being cut, resulting in faster cutting speeds but may produce rougher edges. Both types of machines are widely used in industries such as metal fabrication, construction, and manufacturing.</p></p>
