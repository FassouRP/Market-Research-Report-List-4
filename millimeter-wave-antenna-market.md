<p><p>Millimeter wave antenna market includes two main types - Antenna in Package (AiP) and Antenna on Package (AoP). AiP integrates the antenna within the package of the RF device, providing a compact and efficient solution for high-frequency applications. On the other hand, AoP involves mounting the antenna directly onto the package of the RF device, offering improved performance and signal quality. Both AiP and AoP are key technologies in the millimeter wave antenna market, catering to the growing demand for high-speed wireless communication devices.</p></p>
