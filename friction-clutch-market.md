<p><p>Friction Clutch Market offers two main types: Single Disc Clutch and Double Disc Clutch. Single Disc Clutch consists of a single friction disc, suitable for light-duty applications. Double Disc Clutch has two friction discs, providing higher torque capacity and better heat dissipation, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications. Both types offer efficient power transmission by engaging and disengaging the clutch to control the connection between the engine and transmission, allowing for smooth shifting and optimal performance.</p></p>
