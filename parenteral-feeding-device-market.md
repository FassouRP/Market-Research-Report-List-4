<p><p>Parenteral feeding devices are categorized into two main types: stationary infusion pumps and ambulatory infusion pumps. Stationary infusion pumps are designed for patients who require long-term therapy and need to stay in one location, such as a hospital or home care setting. Ambulatory infusion pumps are portable devices that allow patients to receive their parenteral feeding while on the go, providing them with more independence and freedom to continue with their daily activities. Both types of devices are crucial in providing patients with the necessary nutrition and medication through intravenous administration.</p></p>
