<p><ul><li>Open Wound Drainage System</li><li>Closed Wound Drainage System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wound drainage systems are used to remove excess fluid and air from wounds during the healing process. There are two main types of wound drainage systems: open wound drainage systems and closed wound drainage systems. </p><p>Open wound drainage systems involve a dressing that allows fluid to pass through and be absorbed. Closed wound drainage systems involve a collection device that is attached to a tube inserted into the wound, allowing fluid to be drained into the collection device. Both systems help to prevent infection and promote healing in different ways.</p></p>
