<p><p>Automotive drive belts are essential components in vehicles that help transfer power from the engine to various parts of the vehicle. There are two main types of drive belts in the market: timing belts and serpentine belts. Timing belts regulate the engine's crankshaft and camshaft operation, ensuring proper timing of engine valves. Serpentine belts, on the other hand, power multiple engine components such as the alternator, water pump, and power steering pump. Both types are crucial for the efficient operation of vehicles.</p></p>
