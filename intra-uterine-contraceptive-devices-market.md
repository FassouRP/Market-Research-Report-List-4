There are two main types of IUCDs: Copper IUCDs, which release copper ions to prevent sperm from fertilizing the egg, and Hormonal IUCDs, which release hormones that thicken the cervical mucus and inhibit ovulation. Both types of IUCDs are highly effective in preventing pregnancy and are a popular choice for women looking for long-lasting contraception options.</p></p>
