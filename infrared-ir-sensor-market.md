<p><p>Infrared IR sensors can be categorized into two main types: Thermal Infrared (TIR) Sensor and Quantum Infrared (QIR) Sensor. TIR sensors detect infrared radiation emitted by objects based on their temperature, making them suitable for applications like temperature sensing and night vision. On the other hand, QIR sensors utilize quantum mechanics principles to detect infrared radiation, offering more sensitivity and precision for applications such as spectroscopy and gas analysis. These two types of sensors cater to different market needs and provide unique capabilities for various industries.</p></p>
