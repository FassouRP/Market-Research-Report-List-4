<p><p>Extrusion molding machines are classified into three main types: hydraulic, all-electric, and hybrid. Hydraulic machines use hydraulic systems to generate the force needed for the extrusion process, making them suitable for high-force applications. All-electric machines rely on electric motors for power and are known for their energy efficiency and precise control. Hybrid machines combine the benefits of hydraulic and all-electric systems, offering a balance between power and efficiency. Each type of machine has its own advantages and is used in different applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
