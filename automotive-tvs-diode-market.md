<p><p>Automotive TVS Diodes are devices used to protect sensitive electronic components in vehicles from voltage spikes. There are two main types of TVS diodes - uni-polar and bi-polar. Uni-polar TVS diodes are designed to handle voltage spikes in one direction only, while bi-polar TVS diodes can handle voltage spikes in both directions. Uni-polar TVS diodes are commonly used in applications where voltage spikes are primarily uni-directional, while bi-polar TVS diodes are used in applications where voltage spikes can occur in both directions.</p></p>
