DC Smart PV Combiner Boxes are used to combine the output from multiple strings of solar panels into a single DC output. On the other hand, AC Smart PV Combiner Boxes are designed to combine the DC output from the solar panels with the grid AC power. Both types play a crucial role in optimizing the performance and efficiency of solar PV systems.</p></p>
