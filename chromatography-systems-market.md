<p><p>Chromatography systems are used in various industries for separating and analyzing compounds in a sample. Gas Chromatography (GC) involves separating volatile compounds, while Liquid Chromatography (LC) separates non-volatile compounds. LC-MS combines liquid chromatography with mass spectrometry for more accurate compound identification. GC-MS combines gas chromatography with mass spectrometry for similar purposes. These systems are widely used in pharmaceutical, biotechnology, food and beverage, and environmental industries for quality control, research, and development purposes. Each system offers unique advantages and applications in chemical analysis.</p></p>
