<p><p>A Controllable-Pitch Propeller (CPP) is a type of propeller where the pitch of the blades can be adjusted while the propeller is rotating. CPP systems are commonly used in marine vessels such as ships, ferries, and yachts.</p><p>The Controllable-Pitch Propeller Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.9% during the forecast period.
