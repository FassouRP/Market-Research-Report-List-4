<p><p>Disposable Intravenous Indwelling Needles are commonly classified into two types: Open Indwelling Needle and Closed Indwelling Needle markets. Open Indwelling Needles have a needle that is exposed and connected to an external tubing system, which allows for easy access and manipulation. Closed Indwelling Needles, on the other hand, have a needle that is enclosed within a plastic housing, minimizing the risk of needlestick injuries and contamination. Both types serve the same purpose of providing intravenous access for medication administration and fluid infusion in healthcare settings.</p></p>
