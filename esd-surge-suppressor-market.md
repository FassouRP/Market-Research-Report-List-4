<p><p>ESD surge suppressors are designed to protect electronic devices from damage caused by electrostatic discharge. There are three main types of ESD surge suppressors in the market: Switch Type suppressors, which offer a fast response time to divert surges away from devices; Pressure Limiting Type suppressors, which use gas discharge tubes to absorb and dissipate surges; and Other market suppressors, which may include hybrid suppressors or other specialized technologies for specific applications. Each type has its own advantages and is suited to different situations.</p></p>
