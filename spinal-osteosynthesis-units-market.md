There are three main types of spinal osteosynthesis units based on their approach: posterior, anterior, and lateral. Posterior units are inserted from the back of the spine, anterior units are inserted from the front, and lateral units are inserted from the side. Each type of unit has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs of the patient and the type of spinal injury or condition being treated.</p></p>
