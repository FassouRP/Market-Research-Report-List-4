<p><p>Prefilled auto injectors come in two main types: glass based injectors and plastic based injectors. Glass-based injectors are typically more durable, allowing for higher drug volume and longer shelf life. They are also preferred for drugs that are sensitive to oxidation or light exposure. Plastic-based injectors, on the other hand, are lighter and more cost-effective. They are often used for drugs that do not require long-term storage or are less sensitive to external factors. Both types offer convenient and user-friendly options for self-administration of medication.</p></p>
