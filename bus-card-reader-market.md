<p><p>Bus card readers can be categorized into three main market types based on the technology they use: Contact Technology, Contactless Technology, and NFC Technology. Contact technology requires physical contact between the card and the reader for data transfer, while contactless technology allows data transfer without direct contact. NFC technology utilizes radio frequency identification to enable communication between the card and reader. Each market type offers different advantages such as convenience, speed, and security for transportation systems using bus card readers.</p></p>
