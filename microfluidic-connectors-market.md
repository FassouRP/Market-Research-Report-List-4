<p><p>Microfluidic connectors are essential components in microfluidic systems and are classified into two main types: Circular Microfluidic Connectors and Linear Microfluidic Connectors. Circular connectors allow for fluid flow in a circular manner, whereas linear connectors facilitate straight-line flow of fluids. The choice between these two types depends on the specific requirements of the microfluidic system, such as the complexity of fluid paths and the need for precise control over fluid flow. Both types play a crucial role in enabling efficient and accurate manipulation of fluids in microfluidic devices.</p></p>
