The two main types are On-Pump Transfusion Devices, which are used during surgeries involving the heart-lung machine, and Off-Pump Transfusion Devices, which are used in surgeries performed without the heart-lung machine. Both types help minimize the need for donor blood transfusions, reduce the risk of infection, and promote faster recovery in patients undergoing cardiovascular procedures.</p></p>
