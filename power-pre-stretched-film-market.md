<p><p>Power pre stretched film market includes two main types - coreless power pre stretched film and cored power pre stretched film. Coreless power pre stretched film does not require a core for dispensing, making it more lightweight and cost-efficient. On the other hand, cored power pre stretched film comes with a core for easy dispensing and handling. Both types offer high strength and stretchability, making them ideal for securing and protecting palletized goods during transportation and storage. Customers can choose between these two options based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
