<p><p>Medical isolators are used in healthcare facilities to create a controlled environment for handling hazardous materials like chemicals or pathogens. There are two main types of medical isolators: floor-standing type and mobile type. Floor-standing isolators are stationary units that are often used in labs for long-term projects. Mobile isolators are on wheels, allowing for easy movement between different workspaces. Both types provide a secure and protected environment for conducting experiments or treatments without the risk of contamination.</p></p>
