<p><p>Pulley generators come in different types, with the two main kinds being single wheel generators and two wheel generators. Single wheel generators consist of a single pulley wheel that rotates to generate power. On the other hand, two wheel generators have two pulley wheels that work together to produce electricity. Both types of generators work by using mechanical energy from continuous rotation of the pulley wheels, which is then converted into electrical energy through a generator.</p></p>
