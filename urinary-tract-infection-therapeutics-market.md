<p><p>Urinary tract infection therapeutics market includes various types of medications to treat the infection. Some common types include quinolones, penicillin & combinations, cephalosporin, tetracycline, sulphonamides, nitrofurans, and other medications. Quinolones are antibiotics that work by inhibiting bacterial DNA replication, while penicillin & combinations target the bacterial cell wall. Cephalosporin works similarly to penicillin, disrupting cell wall synthesis. Tetracycline inhibits protein synthesis in bacteria, sulphonamides disrupt folic acid synthesis, and nitrofurans inhibit bacterial DNA replication. Other medications may include alternative antibiotics or urinary analgesics.</p></p>
