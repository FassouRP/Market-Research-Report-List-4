<p><p>3D printing technology in the pharmaceutical market includes various types such as Material Extrusion, Binder Jetting, Powder Bed Fusion, and VAT Photopolymerization. </p><p>Material Extrusion involves layer-by-layer deposition of materials, often in the form of filaments. Binder Jetting uses a binding agent to solidify powdered materials. Powder Bed Fusion melts and fuses materials together with a laser or electron beam. VAT Photopolymerization uses light to solidify liquid resin into a desired shape.</p><p>Each type offers unique advantages and applications in pharmaceutical product development and production.</p></p>
