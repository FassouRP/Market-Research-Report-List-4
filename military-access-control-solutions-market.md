<p><p>Military Access Control Solutions market can be categorized into two main types: Discretionary Access Control (DAC) and Mandatory Access Control (MAC). </p><p>Discretionary Access Control allows users to have control over the access permissions of resources based on their discretion. On the other hand, Mandatory Access Control enforces access restrictions based on predefined security policies set by system administrators, without the ability for users to override.</p><p>Both DAC and MAC are crucial in ensuring the security and integrity of military systems and sensitive information.</p></p>
