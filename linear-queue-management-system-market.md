<p><p>A Linear Queue Management System consists of various market types: Structured Queue, which follows a set order for serving customers; Unstructured Queue, where customers can join any line and wait for service; Kiosk-Based Queue, where customers can select services from a kiosk and join the corresponding queue; and Moving Queue, where customers move from one service point to another in a continuous queue. Each market type offers a different approach to managing queues efficiently and effectively.</p></p>
