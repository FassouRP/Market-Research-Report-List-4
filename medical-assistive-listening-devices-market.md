<p><p>Medical assistive listening devices market includes ear-based hearing aids, bone-anchored hearing aids, and cochlear implants. Ear-based hearing aids amplify sounds for individuals with hearing loss. Bone-anchored hearing aids use a surgically implanted device to transmit sound vibrations directly to the inner ear. Cochlear implants are electronic devices that stimulate the auditory nerve to provide a sense of sound to those with severe hearing loss. These devices cater to different types and degrees of hearing impairment, offering solutions for improved hearing and quality of life.</p></p>
