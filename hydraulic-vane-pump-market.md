<p><p>Hydraulic vane pumps are available in two main market types: variable pump and quantitative pump. Variable pumps allow for adjustable flow rates, making them ideal for applications where varying hydraulic pressure is required. On the other hand, quantitative pumps maintain a constant flow rate, making them suitable for applications where consistent pressure is needed. Both types offer distinct advantages depending on the specific requirements of the hydraulic system, providing options for a wide range of industrial applications.</p></p>
