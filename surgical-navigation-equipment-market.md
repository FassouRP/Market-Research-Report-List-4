<p><strong>The Surgical Navigation Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Surgical Navigation Equipment</li><li>Electromagnetic Surgical Navigation Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surgical navigation equipment refers to devices used during surgery to track the position of surgical instruments or the patient's anatomy in real time. Optical surgical navigation equipment uses cameras and infrared light to create a 3D map of the patient's anatomy, while electromagnetic surgical navigation equipment uses sensors and magnetic fields to track the movements of surgical instruments. Both types of equipment help surgeons accurately navigate the surgical site, leading to improved precision and outcomes in various surgical procedures.</p></p>
