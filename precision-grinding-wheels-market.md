<p><p>Precision grinding wheels are used in various types of grinding processes such as cylindrical external grinding, internal grinding, surface grinding, and centerless grinding. Cylindrical external grinding involves grinding the outer surface of a workpiece, while internal grinding focuses on the inner diameter. Surface grinding is used to create a smooth finish on flat surfaces, and centerless grinding is a method of grinding cylindrical workpieces without the need for a center point. These different types cater to various industrial and manufacturing applications.</p></p>
