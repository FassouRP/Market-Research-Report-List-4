<p><p>Structural bearings are essential components in infrastructure projects as they allow for movement and rotation between different parts of a structure. The main types of structural bearings are elastomeric bearings, pot bearings, spherical bearings, and others. Elastomeric bearings are made of layers of rubber and steel to absorb deformation. Pot bearings utilize a combination of steel and rubber to support heavy loads. Spherical bearings allow for multidirectional movement. Other types include sliding bearings, roller bearings, and disc bearings, each with unique features to accommodate specific structural needs.</p></p>
