Dental implants are artificial tooth roots used to support replacement teeth. Dental bridges are used to fill gaps caused by missing teeth. Crowns are caps that cover damaged or decayed teeth. Dentures are removable prosthetic teeth used to replace missing teeth. Abutments are connectors between dental implants and prosthetic teeth.
