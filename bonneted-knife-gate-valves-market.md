<p><ul><li>Metal Seated Knife Gate Valves</li><li>Resilient Seated Knife Gate Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bonneted knife gate valves come in two main types - Metal seated knife gate valves and Resilient seated knife gate valves. Metal seated knife gate valves are designed for applications with abrasive or high-temperature fluids, providing a tight shut-off with metal to metal sealing. On the other hand, Resilient seated knife gate valves are designed for applications with non-abrasive fluids, offering a bubble-tight shut-off with a soft resilient seat. Both types of valves offer efficient and reliable performance in diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
