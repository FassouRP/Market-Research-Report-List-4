<p><p>Digestible medical sensors are small devices that can be swallowed and used to monitor vital signs and other health parameters within the body. Two common types of digestible medical sensors are Copper-Coating and Magnesium-Coating sensors. Copper-Coating sensors are designed to be biocompatible and can help in detecting changes in the gastrointestinal tract. Magnesium-Coating sensors, on the other hand, are biodegradable and can safely be broken down by the body after use. Both types of sensors offer unique advantages in the field of medical monitoring and diagnostics.</p></p>
