<p><p>Boat steering systems can be classified into four main types, including manual steering, hydraulic steering, electric power steering, and electro-hydraulic steering. Manual steering systems rely on physical strength to turn the boat, while hydraulic systems use fluid pressure to assist with steering. Electric power steering systems use an electric motor to aid in steering, and electro-hydraulic systems combine the benefits of hydraulic and electric power steering for optimal control and precision. Each type of steering system offers unique advantages for different types of boats and navigational needs.</p></p>
