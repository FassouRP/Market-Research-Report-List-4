<p><p>Automotive Over-The-Air Update Market includes two main types - Firmware Over-The-Air Technology (FOTA) and Software Over-The-Air Technology (SOTA). FOTA involves updating the embedded software in electronic control units within a vehicle, while SOTA involves updating the applications and features running on the vehicle's infotainment system. Both technologies allow automakers to remotely update vehicle systems, improve functionality, fix bugs, and enhance cybersecurity, providing convenience for consumers and cost savings for manufacturers.</p></p>
