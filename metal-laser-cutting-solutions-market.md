<p><p>Metal laser cutting solutions market can be divided into various types based on the cutting processes used. These types include Laser Vaporization Cutting, where high-energy laser beams are used to remove material through vaporization; Laser Melting Cutting, where the laser beam heats the material to melting point before a gas jet removes the molten material; Laser Oxygen Cutting, where oxygen gas is used to aid in the cutting process by reacting with the metal; and other markets that utilize different cutting techniques like water jet cutting or plasma cutting.</p></p>
