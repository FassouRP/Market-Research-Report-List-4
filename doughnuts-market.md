<p><ul><li>Cake Style</li><li>Yeast Style</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cake Style doughnuts are denser and more moist, with a crumbly texture. They are typically leavened with baking powder, resulting in a cake-like consistency. They are often flavored with ingredients such as vanilla or chocolate, and can be topped with various types of glazes or sprinkles. Yeast Style doughnuts, on the other hand, are lighter and airy with a chewy texture. They are leavened with yeast, allowing them to rise and create a fluffy interior. Yeast Style doughnuts are often glazed or filled with various creams or fruit fillings.</p></p>
