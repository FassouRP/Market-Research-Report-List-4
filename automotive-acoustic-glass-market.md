<p><p>Automotive Acoustic Glass market consists of two main types - Double Deck Glass and Three Layers of Glass. Double Deck Glass incorporates two panes of glass with a layer of sound-absorbing material between them, providing enhanced noise reduction. On the other hand, Three Layers of Glass consists of three panes of glass with two soundproofing layers, offering superior insulation against external noise. Both types of acoustic glass help improve the overall driving experience by reducing unwanted noise in the vehicle cabin.</p></p>
