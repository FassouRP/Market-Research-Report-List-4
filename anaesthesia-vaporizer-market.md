<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Anaesthesia Vaporizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plenum Vaporizers</li><li>Drawover Vaporizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plenum vaporizers are a type of anaesthesia vaporizer that deliver a precise concentration of anaesthetic gas to the patient by using a pressurized gas source. Drawover vaporizers, on the other hand, rely on the patient's own breathing to draw anaesthetic gas through the vaporizer and into their lungs. Plenum vaporizers are more commonly used in modern anaesthesia machines due to their accuracy and efficiency, while drawover vaporizers are simpler and portable, making them suitable for use in remote or low-resource settings.</p></p>
