<p><ul><li>Time Division Duplex (TDD)</li><li>Frequency Division Duplex (FDD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LTE Small Base Station market can be categorized into Time Division Duplex (TDD) and Frequency Division Duplex (FDD) types. TDD technology allows transmission and reception of data on the same frequency at different times, offering flexible and efficient spectrum usage. On the other hand, FDD technology enables simultaneous transmission and reception of data on separate frequencies, providing better quality and less interference. Both TDD and FDD markets cater to different needs and preferences of LTE small base station users.</p></p>
