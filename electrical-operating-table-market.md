<p><p>Electrical Operating Tables are classified into two main types: Electrical Motor Type and Electrical Hydraulic Type. Electrical Motor Type tables are operated using electric motors for height adjustment, tilting, and other movements. These tables are popular for their smooth and precise positioning capabilities. On the other hand, Electrical Hydraulic Type tables utilize hydraulic systems to control movements, providing strong lifting capabilities and stable positioning. Both types cater to various medical procedures and offer advanced functionalities to assist medical professionals during surgeries and examinations.</p></p>
