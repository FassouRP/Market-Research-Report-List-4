<p><p>ALD equipment market consists of different types based on the deposition method used. PEALD (Plasma-Enhanced ALD) utilizes plasma to enhance the deposition rate and film properties compared to traditional ALD methods. On the other hand, Thermal ALD relies solely on thermal energy to deposit thin films with high precision and control. Both PEALD and Thermal ALD have their unique advantages and applications in various industries such as semiconductors, MEMS devices, and solar panels. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
