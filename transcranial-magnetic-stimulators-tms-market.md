<p><p>Transcranial Magnetic Stimulators (TMS) market consists of four main types: sTMS (Single-pulse TMS), pTMS (Paired-pulse TMS), rTMS (Repetitive TMS), and nTMS (Navigated TMS). sTMS delivers brief magnetic pulses to stimulate nerve cells in specific areas of the brain. pTMS involves delivering two magnetic pulses in rapid succession to investigate interhemispheric inhibition. rTMS delivers repeated pulses to modulate brain activity and treat various neurological disorders. nTMS uses advanced navigation systems to precisely target specific brain regions for diagnostic and therapeutic purposes.</p></p>
