<p><p>Stroke Management refers to the holistic approach to treating and preventing strokes, which are caused by interrupted blood flow to the brain. These technologies allow healthcare professionals to monitor patients remotely, providing timely intervention and reducing the risk of complications. Additionally, there is a growing emphasis on preventive care and lifestyle interventions to reduce the risk factors for strokes, such as hypertension, obesity, and smoking. This holistic approach to stroke management is expected to drive market growth in the coming years.</p></p>
