<p><p>Pipe tracing systems are used to prevent pipes from freezing and maintain the temperature in industrial applications. The two main types of pipe tracing systems are steam tracking and electric tracking. Steam tracking systems use steam to heat the pipes, while electric tracking systems use electric heat cables. Steam tracking systems are typically more efficient and cost-effective for large industrial applications, while electric tracing systems are better suited for smaller installations or where steam is not readily available. Both systems are essential for ensuring smooth operations in cold weather conditions.</p></p>
