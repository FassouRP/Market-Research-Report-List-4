<p><p>The mobile DR market can be classified into three main types: Manual, Electric, and Remote Control Drive. Manual mobile DR systems require physical effort from the operator to move the equipment to the desired location. Electric mobile DR systems use motors for movement, reducing the manual effort required. Remote Control Drive mobile DR systems allow operators to control the movement of the equipment from a distance, providing convenience and flexibility in positioning. Each type offers unique advantages and caters to different user preferences and workflows in medical imaging applications.</p></p>
