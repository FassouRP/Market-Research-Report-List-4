<p><p>Implantable infusion pumps are used for continuous and controlled drug delivery directly into the patient's body. There are two main types of implantable infusion pumps - piston type and creeping type. </p><p>Piston type infusion pumps use a mechanical piston to deliver the drug, while creeping type pumps have a flexible membrane that slowly pushes the drug out. These pumps are designed to provide precise and accurate dosing of medications over an extended period, offering patients a convenient and effective treatment option.</p></p>
