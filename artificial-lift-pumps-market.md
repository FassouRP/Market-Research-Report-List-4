<p><p>Electric Submersible Pump (ESP) is a type of artificial lift pump commonly used in oil and gas production. Rod Pump utilizes a rod string and pump jack to lift fluids to the surface. Progressive Cavity Pump (PCP) is a positive displacement pump suitable for heavy oil and high viscosity fluids. Plunger Lift is used to optimize production in marginal wells. Hydraulic Piston Pump is a hydraulic system used in high-pressure applications. Each type of pump serves a specific purpose in the artificial lift market.</p></p>
