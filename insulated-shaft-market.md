<p><p>An insulated shaft is a mechanical component used in various industries to transmit power between rotating components while isolating them electrically. It consists of a central shaft encased in insulation material to prevent electrical arcing between the components it connects. Insulated shafts are commonly used in machinery and equipment where there is a risk of electrical interference or short circuits.</p><p>The Insulated Shaft Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.5% during the forecast period. The growth of this market can be attributed to the increasing demand for machinery and equipment in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing. The need for improved electrical insulation in rotating components to enhance safety and efficiency is also driving the market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Insulated Shaft Market is the development of advanced insulation materials that offer higher dielectric strength and thermal stability. Manufacturers are also focusing on designing insulated shafts with enhanced durability and reliability to meet the growing demands of various industries.
