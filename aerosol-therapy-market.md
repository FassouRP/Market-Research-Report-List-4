<p><p>Aerosol therapy market includes three main types of devices used for delivering respiratory medication: inhalers, nebulizers, and bronchodilators. Inhalers are handheld devices that deliver a specific dose of medication directly into the lungs, commonly used for asthma and COPD. Nebulizers are devices that convert liquid medication into a fine mist for inhalation through a mask or mouthpiece, often used for children or patients with severe respiratory conditions. Bronchodilators are medications that help relax and open the airways, typically used in combination with inhalers or nebulizers for respiratory conditions.</p></p>
