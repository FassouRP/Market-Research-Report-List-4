<p><p>Benchtop conductivity meters are available in three main types: single channel, dual channel, and multi channel. Single channel meters are designed to measure the conductivity of a single sample at a time. Dual channel meters can simultaneously measure the conductivity of two separate samples. Multi channel meters, on the other hand, have the capability to measure the conductivity of multiple samples at once. Each type of meter offers different levels of efficiency and convenience depending on the user's needs.</p></p>
