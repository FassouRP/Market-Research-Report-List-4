<p><p>Ship unloading hoppers come in three main types: Static Hoppers, Rail Hoppers, and Wheeled Hoppers. Static Hoppers are fixed in place and do not move during the unloading process. Rail Hoppers are mounted on rails and can move along the length of the ship for efficient unloading. Wheeled Hoppers are equipped with wheels for mobility around the dock. Each type of hopper offers unique advantages in terms of flexibility, speed, and convenience in unloading operations.</p></p>
