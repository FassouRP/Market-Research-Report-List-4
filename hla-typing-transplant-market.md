<p><strong>The HLA Typing Transplant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Molecular Assays</li><li>Molecular Assays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HLA typing transplant market includes two main types of assays: non-molecular assays and molecular assays. Non-molecular assays involve methods such as serological testing to determine the compatibility of human leukocyte antigen (HLA) between donor and recipient for organ transplantation. On the other hand, molecular assays utilize DNA analysis techniques like polymerase chain reaction (PCR) to provide a more accurate and detailed HLA typing result. Both types of assays play a crucial role in improving the success rates of organ transplant procedures.</p></p>
