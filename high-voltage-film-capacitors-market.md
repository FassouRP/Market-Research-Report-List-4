Polypropylene Film Capacitors offer excellent thermal stability and low dissipation factor, making them suitable for high voltage circuits. PTFE Film Capacitors are known for their high insulation resistance and low losses at high frequencies. Other types of high voltage film capacitors include polycarbonate film capacitors, which offer high voltage endurance and long-term reliability.</p></p>
