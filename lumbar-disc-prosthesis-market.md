<p><p>Lumbar disc prostheses are medical devices used to replace damaged intervertebral discs in the lumbar region of the spine. There are different types of lumbar disc prostheses available in the market, such as metal-on-metal and metal-on-biopolymer. Metal-on-metal prostheses consist of two metal components that rub against each other, while metal-on-biopolymer prostheses involve a metal component articulating with a biopolymer material. Each type has its advantages and limitations, and the choice between them depends on factors such as patient preference and surgeon recommendation.</p></p>
