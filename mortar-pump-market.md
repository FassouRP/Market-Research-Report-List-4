<p><p>Mortar pumps are commonly categorized into two main types, hydraulic and pneumatic. Hydraulic mortar pumps are powered by hydraulic pressure, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications that require high pressure and output. On the other hand, pneumatic mortar pumps are powered by compressed air, making them more suitable for smaller-scale projects that require flexibility and mobility. Both types of mortar pumps are essential tools in the construction industry, but their specific applications vary based on the project requirements and conditions.</p></p>
