Low viscosity cements are more fluid-like and are suitable for minimally invasive procedures. Medium viscosity cements offer a balance between flowability and strength, making them suitable for various orthopedic surgeries. High viscosity cements are thicker and provide excellent stability and support, making them ideal for complex procedures and for use in weight-bearing bones. Each type offers unique benefits depending on the specific surgical requirements.</p></p>
