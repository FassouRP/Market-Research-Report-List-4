<p><ul><li>2 Pole</li><li>4 Pole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solar isolators are devices used in solar power systems to safely disconnect the power supply. There are two main types of solar isolators available in the market: 2 Pole and 4 Pole isolators. </p><p>2 Pole isolators are used for single-phase systems and have two poles for both positive and negative lines. They are typically used in smaller solar installations. </p><p>4 Pole isolators, on the other hand, are used in three-phase systems and have four poles for each phase along with an additional pole for the neutral line. They are commonly used in larger commercial and industrial solar installations.</p></p>
