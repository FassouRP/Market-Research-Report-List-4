<p><p>The Pneumatic Strapping Tool market includes two main types: Automatic and Semi-automatic tools. Automatic tools are designed to efficiently and quickly strap packages without the need for manual labor. They are ideal for high-volume applications where speed is crucial. On the other hand, Semi-automatic tools require some manual input but still offer faster and more consistent strapping compared to manual tools. Both types cater to different needs, with Automatic tools being more suitable for large-scale operations and Semi-automatic tools being better suited for smaller operations with moderate strapping requirements.</p></p>
