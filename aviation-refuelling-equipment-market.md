<p><p>The aviation refuelling equipment market consists of various types of equipment such as hoses, couplings, nozzles, expansion joints, fuel trucks, and others. Hoses are used to transfer fuel from the storage facility to the aircraft. Couplings connect different sections of hoses or pipelines. Nozzles control the flow of fuel during refuelling. Expansion joints allow for flexibility in pipelines. Fuel trucks transport and dispense fuel at airports. Other equipment includes filters, meters, and valves essential for safe and efficient refuelling operations.</p></p>
