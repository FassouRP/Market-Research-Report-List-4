<p><p>The Deformity Spinal System market includes various types of devices used in spinal surgeries to correct deformities. This includes Rods, Hooks, Plates, Cages, and Pedicle Screws. Rods are used to support and stabilize the spine, while hooks provide additional anchoring points. Plates are used to facilitate fusion and stability, and cages are inserted between vertebrae to promote fusion. Pedicle screws are used to secure rods and plates to the spine, enhancing stability and alignment during fusion procedures.</p></p>
