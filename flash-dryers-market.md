<p><p>Flash dryers are used for drying materials in various industries, with two main market types - direct drying and indirect drying. In direct drying, the material comes into direct contact with hot air or gas to remove moisture quickly. Indirect drying involves heating a surface, such as a metal wall, which then transfers the heat to the material for drying. Both types of flash dryers are efficient and versatile, catering to different drying needs in industries such as food processing, chemical manufacturing, and agriculture.</p></p>
