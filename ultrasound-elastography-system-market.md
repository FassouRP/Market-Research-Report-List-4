<p><p>Ultrasound Elastography System market consists of different types such as Static Elastography (SE), Dynamic Elastography With Harmonic Stimuli (DEHS), and Dynamic Elastography With Transient Stimuli (DETS). Static Elastography measures tissue stiffness without inducing external stimuli. Dynamic Elastography With Harmonic Stimuli uses low-frequency vibrations to assess tissue elasticity. Dynamic Elastography With Transient Stimuli applies short duration impulses to generate shear waves for elastography imaging. Other markets in this category might include Elastography systems using different techniques or technologies to assess tissue elasticity.</p></p>
