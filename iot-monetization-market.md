<p><p>IoT Monetization market is classified into three main types: Private Deployment Model, Public Deployment Model, and Hybrid Deployment Model. The Private Deployment Model is when the IoT infrastructure is deployed within a single organization and its data is kept private. The Public Deployment Model involves deploying IoT infrastructure for public use, with data being shared across multiple organizations. The Hybrid Deployment Model combines aspects of both private and public deployment, allowing for a mix of private and shared data access.</p></p>
