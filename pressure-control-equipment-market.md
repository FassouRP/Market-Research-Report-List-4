<p><p>Pressure control equipment market includes various types of products such as valves, control heads, quick unions, wellhead flanges, and other related items. Valves are used to control the flow of fluids in the well, while control heads regulate the pressure during drilling operations. Quick unions are connectors that facilitate the assembly of different components. Wellhead flanges provide a seal at the wellhead. Other products in the market may include chokes, manifolds, and spools to ensure safe and efficient pressure control in oil and gas operations.</p></p>
