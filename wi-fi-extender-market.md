<p><p>Wi-Fi Range Extenders are devices that amplify the existing Wi-Fi signal in a home or office, extending the network coverage to areas with poor connectivity. They help eliminate dead zones and improve internet speed in far-reaching corners. On the other hand, Wireless AP Routers function as standalone access points that allow devices to connect to a network wirelessly. They are commonly used in settings where multiple devices need to connect to a single network, such as in large homes or offices. Both products serve to enhance and expand Wi-Fi coverage for better connectivity.</p></p>
