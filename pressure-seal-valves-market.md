<p><p>Pressure seal valves are a type of industrial valve designed for high-pressure applications. There are three main types in the market - pressure seal check valves, pressure seal globe valves, and pressure seal gate valves. Pressure seal check valves are used to prevent backflow in pipelines, pressure seal globe valves control flow and pressure, and pressure seal gate valves are used to completely stop or start the flow of fluid. These valves are essential in industries such as oil and gas, power generation, and petrochemicals.</p></p>
