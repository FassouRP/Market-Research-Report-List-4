<p><strong>The AC Voltmeters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Average Reading AC Voltmeters</li><li>Peak Reading AC Voltmeters</li><li>True RMS Reading AC Voltmeters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AC Voltmeters come in three main types: Average Reading AC Voltmeters, Peak Reading AC Voltmeters, and True RMS Reading AC Voltmeters. Average Reading AC Voltmeters provide a simple average of the voltage levels, Peak Reading AC Voltmeters measure the peak value of the voltage signal, and True RMS Reading AC Voltmeters provide an accurate measurement of the root mean square of the voltage signal, making them suitable for non-sinusoidal waveforms. Each type has its own strengths and applications in the market.</p></p>
